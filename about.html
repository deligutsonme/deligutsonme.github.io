About Us


We're a student-led project created to highlight a challenge many of us face but few talk about: limited access to capable devices and reliable internet. This site is part of our effort to make that problem more visible and encourage real solutions.

Our Solution:


We believe in simple, doable steps that make a difference. Here's what we're encouraging:


  • Donate extra laptops, tablets, or Wi-Fi devices
  • Support or start device-lending programs
  • Raise awareness, online, or in your own circles

Because nowadays, being left out because of tech barriers shouldn't be happening anymore.
